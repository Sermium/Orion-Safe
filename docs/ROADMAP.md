# Stellar Vault Roadmap

Our journey to becoming the leading treasury infrastructure on Stellar.

---

## Current Status: Testnet Live ✅

The dashboard and core contracts are deployed on Stellar testnet at:  
🔗 [stellar-vault-eta.vercel.app](https://stellar-vault-eta.vercel.app/)

**What's working today:**
- ✅ Multi-signature vault creation
- ✅ Role-based access control
- ✅ Transaction proposals and approvals
- ✅ Time-lock creation and claiming
- ✅ Vesting schedule management
- ✅ Bulk operations (CSV import)
- ✅ Multi-wallet support
